About L198

Cambodia's fastest-growing e-commerce platform, connecting millions of buyers and thousands of local sellers since 2019.

Our Mission

To empower every Cambodian to buy and sell online with trust, speed, and convenience — building a digital marketplace that reflects the spirit and diversity of Cambodia's vibrant communities.

🇰🇭

Born in Cambodia

Founded in 2019 in Phnom Penh, L198 was built to solve a real local problem: giving Cambodian small businesses a reliable, affordable way to reach buyers nationwide — without needing a physical storefront.

🚀

Rapid Growth

From 200 sellers in our first month to over 18,000 verified stores today, L198 has grown into Cambodia's most trusted online marketplace — processing over 50,000 orders per day across all 25 provinces.

🛡️

Buyer Protection

Every purchase on L198 is covered by our Buyer Protection Guarantee. If your item doesn't arrive or doesn't match the description, we'll make it right — full refund or free replacement, no questions asked.

🌱

Supporting Local

Over 70% of our sellers are small and medium businesses from Cambodia. We provide them with free seller tools, logistics support, and access to marketing programs to help them grow sustainably.

Our Values

Trust First
