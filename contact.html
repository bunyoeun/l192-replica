Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order? ▼
Go to Profile → My Orders and click on your order to see real-time tracking. You'll also receive SMS and in-app notifications at each delivery milestone.
What is L198's return policy? ▼
Most items can be returned within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging. Some categories (food, personal care, digital goods) are non-returnable. Initiate a return from Profile → My Orders → Return/Refund.
What payment methods does L198 accept? ▼
We accept ABA Pay, KHQR, Wing, Visa/Mastercard, and Cash on Delivery (COD). ABA Pay and KHQR offer instant confirmation. COD is available in most areas of Cambodia.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Same-day delivery is available in Phnom Penh for orders placed before 2PM. Next-day delivery covers Siem Reap, Sihanoukville, and other major cities. Provincial areas typically take 2–4 days.
My item arrived damaged — what do I do? ▼
Take photos immediately and initiate a return within 24 hours through the app. Our Buyer Protection covers all damaged items — you'll receive a full refund or free replacement within 3 business days.
How do I become a seller on L198? ▼
Click "Sell on L198" in the footer, create a seller account, and upload your business documents (or just your ID for individuals). Approval takes 24–48 hours. No monthly fees — we only charge a small commission per sale.
